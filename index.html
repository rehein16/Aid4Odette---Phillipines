ABOUT US


Collaboration

Aid4Odette encourages collaboration among donors, local groups, and other stakeholders to achieve common goals and maximize collective effect.

Efficiency

Aid4Odette simplifies the giving process by partnering with highly effective and rigorously verified local organizations, reducing administrative costs while increasing impact.

Sustainability

Aid4Odette welcomes new techniques and technologies to help programs that promote long-term positive change and address the underlying causes of social problems.

Transparency

Aid4Odette provides donors with clear and detailed information about their donation options, the organizations they support, and the impact of their contributions.



Aid4Odette is a nonprofit that supports other nonprofits by connecting them to donors and companies. Since 2023, we've helped trusted, community-led organizations from the Philippines






In 2024, Aid4Odette delivered more than $145 thousand in grants to local charities and relief goods donations in the Philippines.
